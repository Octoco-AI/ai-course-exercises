# Creating your Streakly account

You can sign up for Streakly in two ways.

## Email and password

The most common. Open Streakly, tap **Get started**, pick **Sign up with email**, and enter your email and a password. We send a confirmation link to your email — tap it within 15 minutes, or ask for a new one.

Password requirements:

- At least 10 characters.
- At least one number or symbol.
- We don't enforce capital letters — research shows they don't add security and they frustrate users.

## Sign in with Apple / Google

iOS users can tap **Continue with Apple**. Android users can tap **Continue with Google**. You don't need to choose a password — sign-in handles it.

If you use **Sign in with Apple** and choose "Hide My Email", Streakly will use the relay address. That's fine for us; we don't need your real email.

## Already have an account?

Tap **Sign in** instead of **Get started** on the welcome screen. If you don't remember which method you used:

- Try email first — the app tells you if no account exists.
- Then try Apple / Google — if the account was created this way, the regular email sign-in won't find it.

## I signed up but never got the confirmation email

- Check your spam folder.
- Tap **Resend** from the confirmation screen in the app.
- If still nothing after 10 minutes, the most likely cause is a typo in the email. Tap **Use a different email** and try again.

## My email provider is strict

Some corporate email providers block confirmation emails entirely. If yours does, use **Sign in with Google** or **Apple** instead, or use a personal email address.
