# Plans, billing, and refunds

## Free vs Streakly Plus

**Free** gets you:

- Up to 3 active habits.
- Daily tracking and streak view.
- Local data — nothing syncs between devices.

**Streakly Plus** ($4.99/month or $49/year) adds:

- Unlimited active habits.
- Cross-device sync.
- Home-screen widgets on iOS and Android.
- Weekly insights email.
- Priority support (replies within 1 business day).

The yearly plan saves you about 18% vs monthly.

## How to upgrade

In the app: **Settings → Streakly Plus → Subscribe**. Billing goes through Apple App Store or Google Play Store — we never see your card details. You can cancel from the same screen, and your Plus features stay active until the end of the current billing period.

## Billing cycle

Monthly plans charge on the same day each month. Yearly plans charge on the same day each year. We don't do mid-cycle plan changes — if you upgrade from monthly to yearly, the next charge is the yearly price and the monthly cycle cancels.

## Refunds

Apple and Google handle all refunds directly. We can't issue them from our side. To request:

- **Apple**: [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com). Apple usually approves within 48 hours for recent charges.
- **Google**: Play Store → **Account → Purchase history → Report a problem**.

If you're within 14 days of the charge and haven't used Plus features, both stores typically refund. Older charges or active use lower the chance but we've seen Apple refund after 30 days for compelling reasons.

## I was charged but Plus isn't active

Usually a device-sync delay. Try:

1. Close and reopen the app.
2. Sign out and back in.
3. Settings → Streakly Plus → **Restore purchases**.

If Plus still doesn't activate after all three steps, contact support — we can look at your receipt.

## Canceling Plus

Settings → Streakly Plus → **Cancel subscription**. This opens the App Store / Play Store to confirm; Streakly can't cancel for you.

You keep Plus until the end of the paid period. After that, you're dropped back to Free. If you have more than 3 active habits when that happens, all existing habits remain readable but you can only track the oldest 3 going forward until you either pause the extras or re-subscribe.
