# Contacting support

## How to reach us

**Email**: `support@streakly.app`. This is the main channel — we reply to every message.

- Plus users get priority: we reply within 1 business day.
- Free users: we reply within 3 business days on average.

Direct emails reach us faster than the in-app form because the form adds a step where we have to look up your account. Include your account email in the message body if you email directly.

## What to include in a support email

Help us help you faster:

1. **Your account email.** Without it we can't find you.
2. **Device and OS version.** "iPhone 14, iOS 18.2" or "Pixel 8, Android 15."
3. **App version.** Settings → About → shown at the bottom.
4. **What you were trying to do.**
5. **What happened instead.**
6. **Screenshots or screen recordings.** More is usually better than less.

## What we can and can't help with

**We can:**

- Account recovery (within reason — see `account-security.md`).
- Data export requests beyond the in-app feature.
- Restoring deleted habits within 14 days.
- Investigating bugs affecting your account.
- Clarifying billing questions where you're confused about what you were charged.
- Walking you through feature usage.

**We can't:**

- Issue refunds directly. Apple and Google handle those. See `billing-and-plans.md`.
- See your password (stored as an argon2 hash — we genuinely can't).
- Access your data without your permission.
- Restore data older than 14 days.
- Make product-roadmap commitments in a support email.

## Response expectations

- **Acknowledgement**: within 1 hour during business hours (9–5, US Eastern). We don't always have a full answer that fast but you'll know we saw your message.
- **First real reply**: within 1 business day for Plus, 3 for Free.
- **Follow-up on complex issues**: every 2 business days until resolved.

If you haven't heard from us within the expected window, reply to your original thread to bump it. Don't open a new one — it starts a fresh clock.

## Complaints and escalation

If a reply doesn't resolve your issue, reply with "escalate" and the reason. Your thread goes to the head of support within 1 business day.

If that also doesn't resolve it, email `feedback@streakly.app` with a summary. That goes to the founder. No promises, but every message is read.

## What happens when you write in

A human reads your email. A human replies. We are not a huge team — three people handle support rotating daily. We don't use a ticketing system that hides you behind a queue; your reply goes to a real inbox and your next reply continues the same thread.

This has limits: response times can stretch when one of us is on holiday or a release week is busy. We'd rather be honest about the wait than promise a fake SLA.

## Don't email us for

- **Password resets.** Use the forgot-password link in the app. It's faster.
- **Sign-out-everywhere.** Do it from Settings — instant.
- **Cancelling Plus.** App Store / Play Store — we can't cancel for you.
- **Feature requests.** Use Settings → Feedback in the app; those go to a dedicated board.
